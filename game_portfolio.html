Something about me

I am an Computer Science Engineering graduate from Mumbai with about 4 and half years of work experience at Corporate IT giant and have received various company awards for my work there. Making games is a natural evolution to the passion of games which evolved from playing games to trying to put out my ideas into games and let other play.

Crystal Snow World

This is a twine game with heavy emphasis on user choices. The story is about a doll trapped in a snowball and her journey through the outer world made by humans after she discovers it.

The game has explicit pauses to allow the player to read the text and feel its emotions. Please wait till the page changes or selection options appear.

Please note: This game is still work under progress.

Narrative Game pitch

This is a game pitch/concept with narrative where a drone from Earth crash lands on to an alien planet where the life form is based out of wood. A family on this wood planet meets this drone and unflurs a series of events through the game. This game also explores a newer way of using camera as a game mechanic.
